Global Game Jam 2022 group project:
Brave Magnet

Click here to view the Global Game Jam page

This game was made as part of a collaboration between Rochester Institute of Technology and Kyoto Computer Gakuin in Japan, for Global Game Jam 2022. Our team was comprised of 3 American RIT students, and 4 Japanese KCG students.

For this project, despite my Japanese ability being only somewhat intermediate, I was the person on the team who was most confident with both English and Japanese. Because of this, I took on a bit of a coordinator role. I helped facilitate communication between both sides, and also helped the Japanese students through git operations that they weren't confident with. I also performed some VFX particle work with Niagara.
